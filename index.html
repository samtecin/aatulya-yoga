About Aatulya Yoga

Blending Ancient Traditions with Modern Wellness

Aatulya Yoga blends ancient traditions with modern wellness. Our certified instructors guide you through Hatha, Vinyasa, and meditation practices to harmonize body, mind, and spirit.

Founded in 2010 in the yoga capital of the world, Rishikesh, we've been helping students from around the globe discover the transformative power of yoga. Our approach combines physical postures (asanas), breath control (pranayama), and meditation to create a holistic practice.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced practitioner, our classes are designed to meet you where you are and help you progress at your own pace.
